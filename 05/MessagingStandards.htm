Semantic Interoperabilty and Data Mining in Biomedicine

Summer-school 2005

After the successful first summer school  in 2004 Balatonfüred, in this year the second   Semantic Interoperabilty and Data Mining in Biomedicine Summer School will be organised in a similar beautiful environment in Tihany. This historical village is located next to Balatonfüred on a peninsula named same as the village. The hotel complex (Hotel Park) consisting of three building just side by side is located just at the lake.
