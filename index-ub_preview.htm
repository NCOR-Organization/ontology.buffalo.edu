University at Buffalo to offer a fully online graduate degree in ontology

Search any major employment website and you’re likely to find multiple occurrences of the word “ontology.” But subsequently searching for universities that offer degrees in ontology will return a single result: the University at Buffalo. Beginning with the spring 2026 semester, UB will launch a fully online Master of Science degree (MS) in applied ontology. A sister program at the PhD level will be announced in due course. Both programs are housed within the university’s Department of Philosophy. Both will involve elective courses in Computer Science, Linguistics, Management, and Biomedical Informatics.
