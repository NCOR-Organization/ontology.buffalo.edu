Ontological Spring II

Schloss Dagstuhl – 21-24 May 2006

 

 

 

organized by Barry Smith, IFOMIS (Institute for Formal Ontology and Medical Information Science, Saarland University, Germany),

 

in collaboration with the European Network of Excellence SemanticMining
and the Ontology Outreach Advisory of the European Network of Excellence KnowledgeWeb.

 

A three-day course in ontology will be offered at Schloss Dagstuhl in the Spring of 2006.

 

This event is a sequel to the first Ontological Spring held in 2002 and is conceived very much in the same spirit, as a high-level, intensive overview of theories and tools of ontology. It is however independent of its precursor event. Topics to be covered will include:

 

Domains:                    ontologies of human behaviour, skills and competencies,

ontologies of social institutions,

ontologies of biomedicine

Quality assurance:      quality measures and best practices for ontologies,

registration and certification of ontologies

 

We are targeting advanced doctoral students, but welcome interested post-doc and industrial participants as well. The number of participants is restricted to about 30 to maximize learning possibilities.

 

Faculty will include:

Prof. Barry Smith, IFOMIS, Saarland University, Germany

Dr. Werner Ceusters, ECOR, Saarland University, Germany

Prof. Robert Meersman, STARLab, Free University of Brussels, Belgium

plus others, to be announced.
