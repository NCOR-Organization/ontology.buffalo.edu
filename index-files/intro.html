The Buffalo Ontology Site contains information on ontology, on the history of ontology, and on contemporary ontology and its applications. It will provide links to other ontology sites and announcements of relevant conferences and publications. 

You may begin to browse the site by selecting one of the options from our Main Menu, which is located on the upper right hand corner or by going here .
