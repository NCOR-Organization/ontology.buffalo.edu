APPLIED ONTOLOGY
    Registration Information
     

    Conference fee: $60 before March 30, $75 thereafter.

    This includes:

    • all sessions
    • lunch, dinner and reception Friday and Saturday
    • coffee breaks
    • copy of conference volume

    All currently registered SUNY students may attend the conference free of charge, but must submit the registration form before March 30. There will be a charge for meals.

    Venue:
    Registration and first day of conference will be held at the Center for Inquiry, 1310 Sweet Home Rd. Amherst, NY
    Second day of conference will be at the Center for Tomorrow, UB Amherst Campus, Flint Road.


    Driving Instructions:

    Center for Inquiry

    From US 90 take 290 West
    Exit at Millersport Hwy. North
    Turn at next left, Flint Rd.
    Turn left at Maple Rd.
    Turn right at Sweet Home Rd.
    Center for Inquiry is approximately 1/4 mile on left
     

    Center for Tomorrow

    From US 90 take 290 West
    Exit at Millersport Hwy. North
    Turn left at Flint Rd.
    Cross Maple, stay on Flint into UB campus
    Turn left at first parking lot.
