Barry Smith

Two Video Courses in Ontology

From Aristotle to the Universal Core: An Introduction to Ontology

This course is designed to be of interest to both philosophers and those with a background in computer and information science. No prior knowledge of ontology is presupposed. It is free for use in any way.

Introduction to Biomedical Ontologies

This course provides a basic introduction to ontology with special reference to applications in the field of biomedical research. It is designed to be of interest to both philosophers and those with a background in the life sciences. It is free for use in any way.
