The Center for Inquiry is located on the western boundary of the North Campus of SUNY Buffalo, at 1310 Sweet Home Road. Directions: From highway 290 exit at Millersport North. Turn left at first traffic light onto Flint, turn left at first intersection onto Maple, turn right at first intersection onto Sweet Home. Watch for red sign "Center for Inquiry” immediately after an ugly white truck. The Center is set back on the left after 400 yards and before Rensch Road. From UB North Campus take Rensch Road exit and turn left onto Sweet Home. The Center for Inquiry will be on your right before the highway bridge.
