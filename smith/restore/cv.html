BARRY SMITH

    Curriculum Vitae

    Education

    B.A., Oxford University, 1973. First Class Honors in Mathematics and Philosophy. 

    Ph.D., University of Manchester, 1976, dissertation entitled: The Ontology of Reference. Studies in Logic and Formal Ontology , xvi + 477pp. (a study of theories of meaning and reference in Frege, Husserl and Dummett).

    M.A., Oxford University, 1977.

    Current Research

    Formal Ontology. Applied Ontology (especially: Ontology of Medicine, Ontology of Geography, Ontology of Biology, Ontology and Information Systems Science).

    Theory of Truth and Truthmaking. Metaphysical Applications of Topology and Mereology.

    Austrian and German Philosophy, especially Brentano, Husserl, Reinach, Ingarden.

    Editor

    The Monist . An International Journal of General Philosophical Inquiry (1991­). (Indexed in The Philosophers Index and in Arts and Humanities Citation Index.)
