Why Applied Ontology?

“Ontology” now appears across job listings in government, tech, finance, and biomedicine. UB’s MS in Applied Ontology—delivered fully online—prepares you to design and implement ontology-driven solutions that make data and software work together in the real world.

Applied ontology uses well-defined concepts and relations to structure data and systems so they interoperate—across departments, institutions, and industries.

What you’ll learn

  • Ontology design, evaluation, and governance.
  • Knowledge graphs and AI alignment with ontologies.
  • Enterprise and biomedical interoperability.
  • Standards, data integration, and tooling.

Career outcomes

Ontologist · Knowledge Engineer · Data/AI Ontology Specialist · Information Architect. Sectors include government & intelligence, banking/finance, healthcare & pharma, e-commerce, and manufacturing.
